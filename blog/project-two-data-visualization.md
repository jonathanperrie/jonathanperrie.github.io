# Project Two: Bringing Data to Life with Visualization

This project focused on creating an interactive data visualization dashboard for a large dataset of global economic indicators. The goal was to make complex data accessible and understandable to a non-technical audience.

## The Challenge

The dataset contained millions of records across hundreds of variables. The main challenge was to present this information in a way that was not overwhelming and allowed users to discover insights on their own.

## Our Approach

We chose D3.js for its power and flexibility in creating custom visualizations. Our design process involved creating multiple prototypes and conducting usability tests to refine the user interface.

### Key Visualizations:
- **Interactive World Map:** A choropleth map to visualize country-specific data.
- **Time Series Charts:** Line charts to show trends over time for selected indicators.
- **Scatter Plots:** To explore relationships between different variables.
- **Dynamic Filtering:** Users can filter the data by region, income level, and time period.

## Outcome

The final dashboard provides a powerful tool for exploring global economic data. It has been used by researchers, students, and policymakers to gain insights into global trends. The interactive nature of the visualizations encourages exploration and discovery.
